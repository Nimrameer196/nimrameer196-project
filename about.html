Professional Profile

My engineering journey has been built around solving real-world mechanical challenges through structured design methodologies and computational analysis. I specialize in developing products from concept to production by integrating design engineering, simulation, and manufacturing principles into every stage of development.

Whether designing industrial equipment, optimizing manufacturing processes, or validating mechanical systems through simulation, my objective remains the same: to create reliable, efficient, and high-performance engineering solutions.

Areas of Expertise

From initial concept generation to detailed manufacturing drawings, I develop products that prioritize functionality, manufacturability, and reliability.

I produce detailed 3D models, assemblies, technical drawings, and design documentation using industry-standard CAD software. My workflow emphasizes design accuracy, tolerance control, and clear communication between design and manufacturing teams.

Simulation plays a central role in my design process. I perform structural, thermal, and mechanical analyses to evaluate performance, predict failure modes, and optimize designs before manufacturing, reducing development time and engineering costs.

My industrial experience has provided valuable insight into manufacturing operations, production planning, quality assurance, and continuous improvement. I focus on designing solutions that are not only technically sound but also practical to manufacture and scale.
